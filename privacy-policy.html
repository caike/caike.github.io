Privacy Policy for SWODS App

Effective Date: August 2024

1. Introduction

In SWODS App, your privacy is our priority. We are committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and share data related to your exercise activities.

2. Information We Collect

We collect and process the following information:

Personal Information: This includes your name, email address, and other contact details you provide when creating an account.

Exercise Data: This includes details about your workouts, exercise routines, and fitness goals.

Third-Party Data: We may receive additional data from Garmin via their API to enhance your user experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

- Provide and improve our services, ensuring they meet your fitness needs.

- Personalize your experience, including workout recommendations and progress tracking.

- Connect to Garmin services to sync your exercise data and enhance the overall functionality of the app.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except as necessary to connect with Garmin's API. We ensure that all data sharing is done securely and in compliance with applicable laws.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. This includes encryption, secure storage, and regular security audits.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. Please contact us if you wish to exercise these rights.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes via email or through the app.
